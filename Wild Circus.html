Wild Circus

Since 1893

A world full of stars

"Everyone is a star and deserves the right to twinkle." Marilyn Monroe

When John and Rosemary Wild played their first acrobatic performance, it was only for their family and friends. The more they played the bigger the audience was. In 1893 they used all the savings they had to create a small circus. Now, a bit more than a whole century later, the Wild Circus has been touring in 47 countries around the world and attracted more than 4 000 000 visitors. We thank you very very much for following us in any season and any weather, wherever we are !

Now it is time for you, fellow Wilder, to check out our new performances !

A Dance With Butterflies

Come and enjoy a brand new amazing performance, mixing acrobatics and dance with dozens of butterflies ! Christina, Julia and Ruth will run the show until your eyes pop out of their sockets. A must see !

The Burning Beings

The Beings come from an other world. Waking from the dark, they are snicking every night into our show for a pure moment of dazzling magic ! Children will love it !

The Flying Angels

Your heart will sink when, far from the ground, the Angels will be flying, whirling, twirling over your head. An impressive romantic story, along with a special guest on the piano !



It took hard work and a lot of time to make these things happen. We hope you'll enjoy the show !
